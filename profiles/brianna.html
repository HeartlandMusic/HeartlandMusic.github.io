Brianna Boyko

Brianna Boyko is a passionate country singer from Edmonton who has performed from a variety of venues such as CCMA's at the Westin Hotel, Leduc Black and Gold Rodeo, Angel Flight Fundraiser (with Dash Riprock), The Almanac, The Longest Hockey Game, Chilli Cookoff for Kids with Cancer, Alberta 55+ Senior Games, Tour of Alberta, Party in the Park, Barnyard Bash, Northside Vibe, and Ivan Daines Pick - Nic. She has performed on the ATB Homegrown Talent Stage at Big Valley Jamboree three years in a row. Brianna also sang in the choir at Martin Kerr’s concert at the Francis Winspear Centre for Music. Brianna’s love of country music has allowed her the opportunity to have originals written for her by Sule Heitner and Elizabeth MacInnis. “Tell You What” written by Elizabeth MacInnis is Brianna’s new single, which hit iTunes, Spotify and many more July 2017. Brianna is proud to be the lead singer in “Heartland” and is ecstatic for their future endeavors. In her spare time Brianna plays the fiddle and Ukrainian dances.
