Martin Bui

Our drummer Martin Bui is currently enrolled in the Bachelor of Music program at the University of Alberta with the intent of pursuing a career in performance and education. He’s performed at the Vancouver Symphony Orchestra Institute 2019, the Francis Winspear Center for Music, the Vic Lewis festival in Canmore, and the SunPeaks Cantando festival, to name a few. He’s performed in genres ranging from classical to metal over the course of 11 years, and he does clinics and lessons independently.
